About

Seven days of Awesomeness!

18-24June 2015, Hyderabad

  • Swecha is conducting a 7 day summer camp for the technical aspirants to move from basic towards advanced level technicaltraining for cutting edge technologies to stand up in the current evolving trend.
  • The main objective of this camp is to introduce and work with the cutting edge technologies under the guidance of experts from industry.

    • Speakers

    The experts in the industry will introduce you to cutting-edge technologies and guide you through implementing them.

    Practicums

    These workshops will give you hands-on experience with the technolgies. They will give you a chance to apply what you learn to attain a deeper understanding of the topic.

    Social Skills

    This event will enable you to expand your network and meet people with similar interests, potentially creating new, exiting oppurtunities for you.
